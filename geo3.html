Introduction

The Earth is the only planet in the Solar System that has life. Like land and water, air is also a significant component for the survival of life on the Earth. We cannot live in the absence of air. We cannot feel air when it is calm,but air can be felt while it is in motion.
The atmosphere is the thin layer of air that surrounds earth. It is like ablanket of air that protects earth from the harmful rays of the Sun. It also controls the temperature and most importantly it contains oxygen whi ch is essential for every form of life..
