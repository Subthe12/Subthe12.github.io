Maps

A map is a representation or drawing of the Earth's surface or a part of its area drawn on a flat surface. It is always made according to a scale.
The word map comes from two Latin words, "mappa" which means "sheet" and "mundi" which means "of the world". it is called so as in erlier times all the maps were drawn on sheets, clothes or skins of animals.


Types of Maps

1) Physical Maps: They show physical features like mountains, rivers, natural vegetitation, soil types, etc.

2) Political maps: They emphasise a particular theme or special topic such as the average distribution of rainfall in an areea, population density in a country, etc.

3) thematic maps: They emphasise a particular theme or special topic such as the population in a country, etc.
