Definition of Weather and Climate


Weather is defined as the state of the atmosphere over a short period of time. It can change over the time. In simple terms it refer s to the day-to-day conditions of the atmosphere of a definite place and time.
Climate refers to the atmospheric conditions over a long period of time. It can be defined as the average weather conditions prevailing at a place and recorded over a long time.The time perion varies from 30-35years.

Elements of Weather and Climate

The elements that determine the weather and climate are:
  1. Temperature
  2. Atmospheric Pressure
  3. Wind
  4. Humidity
  5. Cloudliness
  6. Precipitation
